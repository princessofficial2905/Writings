The Apologies
I Never Received but still deserved.

Written by Princess  ·  3 min read

Read
✦

There comes a moment in your healing when you finally admit that there are people who should have apologized to you… but never did. People who hurt you with their silence, their carelessness, their indifference, their broken promises, their disappearing, their sharp words, their emotional neglect. You didn't want revenge. You didn't want them to suffer. All you ever wanted was acknowledgment, a simple, honest, heartfelt, "I'm sorry. You didn't deserve that." But that apology never came. And for the longest time, you carried the weight of that absence like a quiet bruise inside your soul, believing that maybe the lack of apology was somehow a reflection of your worth, when in reality, it was only a reflection of their character.

Their lack of apology wasn't your burden. It was their limitation.

It took time, long, emotional, painful time, for you to understand that some people don't apologize not because you didn't deserve it, but because they lack the emotional courage to face themselves. They hide behind silence because accountability feels too heavy. They pretend nothing happened because truth makes them uncomfortable. They walk away without closure because closure requires honesty they don't have. And while you were losing sleep over the pain they caused, they were running away from the mirror that would force them to confront their actions. Their lack of apology wasn't your burden, it was their limitation.

✦

But even though they never said the words you needed, God heard them on your behalf.

He saw the unfairness.

He saw the hurt you never deserved.

He saw the nights you replayed moments hoping to understand what you did wrong.

"You were not the one who needed to apologize."

God doesn't wait for people to say sorry to begin healing you. He starts mending your heart the moment you hand Him the pieces, even when the world denies you the acknowledgment you deserved. His justice is quiet but complete. His comfort is gentle but powerful.

And here is the most beautiful truth

You don't need their apology to move on. You don't need their words to validate your pain. You don't need their guilt to confirm your worth. You don't need their closure to create your freedom.

Your healing is not in their hands, it is in yours, and in God's. One day, you will look back and realize that the apology you never received became the very thing that taught you how to validate your own heart. It taught you how to protect your boundaries, how to honor your emotions, how to walk away without begging for accountability from someone who could barely be honest with themselves.

People who heal with their presence instead of hurting with their silence.

And soon, my friend, you will meet people who do apologize. People who take responsibility. People who value your feelings. People who know how to say, "I was wrong, and I'm sorry," because they care more about your heart than their ego. People who heal with their presence instead of hurting with their silence.

✦

You deserved every apology you never got.

But what you're about to receive will make up for all of it, with love so genuine, so tender, so emotionally honest that you will finally feel what it's like to be cherished the way you always should have been.

✦
