Leaving
Didn't
Hurt Me

Realizing how long I stayed did.

A Meditation on Love & Letting Go

Scroll to read

There comes a moment when you look back at all the times you stayed, stayed in love, stayed in hope, stayed in confusion, stayed in pain, and you finally admit to yourself that your heart deserved to let go long before you actually did. You stayed because you loved deeply. You stayed because you believed things could change. You stayed because you saw potential instead of patterns. You stayed because you didn't want to give up on someone you once prayed for. But now, with clearer eyes and a softened heart, you can finally see the truth: staying wasn't strength… letting go would've been.

You stayed during the moments when your heart whispered, "You're hurting." You stayed when their actions didn't match their words. You stayed when you felt lonely beside someone who claimed to care. You stayed when you were giving everything and receiving almost nothing. You stayed because the thought of letting go scared you more than the pain of holding on. But what you didn't realize then was that every moment you stayed past your peace was a moment you were abandoning yourself.

God saw the way you fought for something that wasn't fighting for you. He saw the tears you hid behind silent smiles. He saw you begging the universe for a sign, even while your intuition was screaming the answer. He saw how loyal your heart was, even to places that kept breaking it. And gently, lovingly, He began to separate you from what was wounding you. Not because you were weak, but because you deserved a love that didn't require self-sacrifice to feel seen.

✦   ✦   ✦

One day, My friend, you will look back at the version of yourself who stayed too long and feel nothing but compassion for her.

She wasn't clueless, she was hopeful.
She wasn't stuck, she was loyal.
She wasn't weak, she was loving with everything she had.
She stayed because she didn't yet know that letting go is sometimes the highest form of self-love.

And when the right love arrives, a love that stays without being asked, that chooses you without hesitation, that meets you fully and consistently, you will understand why God didn't let you stay where your heart kept breaking.

You will understand that letting go wasn't losing. It was making room. It was clearing emotional space for the love, peace, and tenderness that were always meant for you.

The moments you stayed
taught you your worth.

The moment you let go
will change your life.

Princess

Just a girl named Princess… dreaming of that one soul who stumbles upon her words and finally feels less alone.
